# Phase 2.1: Document Intelligence / OCR Use Cases

## Simple
1. **Prescription Label OCR** - Extract drug name, dosage, instructions from printed pharmacy labels
2. **Insurance Card Scanning** - Capture member ID, group number, payer from card images  
3. **Patient Intake Forms** - Digitize standard demographic forms

## Moderate
4. **EOB Processing** - Parse itemized charges and patient responsibility
5. **Medical Records Requests** - Extract identifiers from faxed request forms
6. **Lab Requisition Forms** - Capture test orders and ICD codes

## Complex
7. **Handwritten Clinical Notes** - Digitize physician progress notes
8. **Historical Chart Migration** - Bulk conversion of legacy paper records
9. **Claims Attachment Processing** - Multi-document classification and extraction
